WHAT IF THE ORDER DOES NOT HAVE A PROPERTY MARK?

IF THE ORDER DOES NOT HAVE A PROPERTY MARK THEN IT WILL BE CONSIDERED LINE GOODS OJG. ALL GOODS WITH NO PROPERTY MARK SHOULD FOLLOW OJG PACKING INSTRUCTIONS.****

WHAT IF THE GOODS ARE PROPERTY MARKED FOR HUC?
